Three of the Best Pictures Worth Watching Acccording to Bill

My Top Picks are:

Sleepless in Seattle

Sleepless in Seattle is a 1993 American romantic comedy-drama film.
It stars Tom Hanks and Meg Ryan. The film follows a journalist who,
despite being newly engaged, becomes enamored with a recently widowed
architect. The architect's son calls a NY radio talk program requesting
a new partner for his grieving father. The broadcast content is so sincere
that the journalist who lives in NY makes the trip to meet the father and his son.

Goldfinger

Goldfinger is a 1964 spy film and the third instalment in the James Bond
series starring Sean Connery as the fictional MI6 007 agent James Bond.

The Thing from Another World

The Thing from Another World, sometimes referred to as just "The Thing"
is a 1951 American black-and-white science fiction-horror film,
The film stars Margaret Sheridan, Kenneth Tobey, Robert Cornthwaite, and
Douglas Spencer. James Arness plays The Thing. As the first introduction to
Sci-fi drama and horror the movie makes a lasting impression.
